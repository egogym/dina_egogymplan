Meal Preparation Tips

  • • Prep meals in advance when possible
  • • Use food scale for accurate portions
  • • Store meals properly in containers
  • • Focus on whole, unprocessed foods

Timing Guidelines

  • • Space meals 3-4 hours apart
  • • Eat within 1 hour post-workout
  • • Have protein with each meal
  • • Adjust portions based on hunger

Supplements & Hydration

Daily Supplements

  • • Whey Protein: 1-2 scoops daily
  • • Multivitamin: 1 tablet with breakfast
  • • Fish Oil: 1000mg with any meal
  • • Creatine: 5g daily (optional)

Hydration Guidelines

  • • Minimum 2.5-3 liters water daily
  • • Extra 500ml during workouts
  • • Monitor urine color (light yellow)
  • • Limit caffeine after 4 PM
