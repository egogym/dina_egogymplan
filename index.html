Program Overview

This comprehensive training plan is designed for muscle gain, featuring a mix of compound exercises, isolation movements, and strategic cardio sessions. The program follows an upper/lower split with active recovery days for optimal results.

Training Guidelines

Key Points

  • Always complete warm-ups to prepare muscles and joints
  • Adjust cardio intensity based on energy levels
  • Include at least 2 rest or active recovery days weekly
  • Focus on progressive overload for muscle growth

Recovery Tips

  • Get 7-9 hours of sleep per night
  • Stay hydrated throughout the day
  • Maintain proper nutrition
  • Listen to your body's signals

Comprehensive Nutrition & Supplement Guidelines

Daily Supplements

  • Protein Powder: 20-25g post-workout
  • Multivitamin: 1 tablet daily
  • Omega-3: 1000mg daily
  • Creatine: 5g daily (optional)

Nutrition Guidelines

  • Water: 2-3 liters daily
  • Post-workout meal: Within 1-2 hours
  • Balanced nutrition with adequate protein
  • Consistent meal timing
